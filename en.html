Welcome to Produkson
your one stop shop for sound & lights

Whether organizing a conference, wedding, party, festival, concert, assembly, tournament or any other event, big or small, we’ll contribute to making it a fun and enriching experience.

Produkson has a large inventory of cutting-edge equipment always ready for deployment. We guarantee rigorous maintenance and a particular attention to detail during installation for a flawless event.

Either for rental or a complete turnkey solution, including delivery, installation and operation, we offer all necessary equipment to meet your needs.
  • Audio Gear such as Speakers, mixers, wireless microphones, in-ear monitors, D.I's, snakes etc.
  • Ligthing Gear (Moving ligths, Beams, strobes, fog and haze, consoles, mapping softwares and more)
  • Video Gear (HD projectors, large venue projections, Screens, monitors etc.)
  • Power distribution Gear (Distribution pannels, cam-lock cablings, dimmers, quad boxs etc.)

You have your own gear and need someone to operate? We can help! We offer our expertise in design and event management.
